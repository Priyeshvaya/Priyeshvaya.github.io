Email Marketing Solutions

Email is the best way to quickly reach out with an update for your customers.

The latest product launch, your next big event, and any news about your business are all great topics for regular newsletters. If a customer has opted into receiving emails from you then you should not be shy about actually sending out emails and making contact with them.

Consider that most people check their phone to see their email updates before they even get out of bed. What if your business could be one of the first things someone thinks about tomorrow before they have rolled out of bed?

Email marketing can deliver that. 

CLICK TO LEARN MORE

Email marketing is...

  1. HIGHLY TARGETED - It is best for securing repeat business because it consists of your existing customers who want to hear from you! Most people do not change their email address, even after 20 years, so you can always reach them!
  2. BUDGET FRIENDLY - You pay per campaign, not per email sent. You avoid printing and advertising costs as well.
  3. VISUALLY APPEALING - You can add images and videos which make your email even more effective, and it smart resizes to any device.
  4. EFFECTIVE EVEN IF THEY DON'T OPEN YOUR EMAIL - Because they will still see the subject line and remember your brand.

E-mail marketing is better than...

Instagram/Facebook

The messaging on social platforms is scattered rather than highly targeted.

Second, thanks to changes in the platform algorithms, your target audience is unlikely to see your message unless you pay for advertising.

SMS

SMS marketing is old-fashioned, expensive, and only contains text as opposed to the rich media capabilities offered by email. Not to mention, it is very obtrusive and very irritating for your customers! Do you want to be remembered as the company that is always annoying them?

Whatsapp

WhatsApp messages give the impression of being a low-budget option instead of a classy communication from a well-reputed brand. Not to mention they are just as annoying and obtrusive as SMS.

Flyers

Distributing flyers is expensive and ineffective. Do you think your customers will save your flyer and look for it when they need your services? With an email, they can simply run a search in their inbox. 

Billboards

Billboards are an expensive marketing tool and only effective while your ad is up. It is unlikely your customers will remember you when they actually need your services if your billboard is no longer there.
